# Public Defender Quick-Start Guide

**Time to read: 5 minutes**

Welcome to Partner! As a public defender, you help debtors understand their situations and navigate the debt resolution process.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Accept Your Invitation

1. Check your email for an invitation from Partner
2. Click the registration link
3. Verify it shows your email and organization

### Step 2: Create Your Account

Fill in your registration form:
- First Name, Last Name
- Phone (optional)
- Bar Number (required)
- Bar State (select from dropdown)
- Password (min 8 chars, 1 uppercase, 1 number)

### Step 3: Complete Onboarding

After registration, complete three steps:

1. **Upload Credentials**
   - BAR Card (required)
   - Photo ID (required)
   - Organization Letter (optional)

2. **Complete Training**
   - Review required training modules
   - Mark each as complete when done

3. **Accept Terms**
   - Read the Terms of Service
   - Check the acceptance box
   - Click "Accept & Complete"

---

## Your Dashboard

Once active, your dashboard shows:

### Summary Cards
- **Active Cases** - Cases you're currently handling
- **Needs Attention** - Cases requiring action
- **Completed** - Resolved cases
- **Pending Consent** - Awaiting debtor consent

### Quick Actions
- View All Assignments
- My Profile
- Messages (coming soon)

---

## Managing Assignments

### Viewing Your Cases

1. Click **"My Assignments"** in the navigation
2. See all your assigned cases
3. Filter by status if needed

### Opening a Case

1. Click any case in your assignment list
2. View debtor information and case details
3. Use breadcrumbs to navigate: Dashboard > Assignments > Case

---

## Viewing Case Details

When you open a case, you'll see:

- **Debtor Information** - Name, contact, financial situation
- **Debt Details** - Amount, creditor, timeline
- **Communication History** - Messages between parties
- **Notes** - Your private case notes

---

## Navigation

Your sidebar includes:
- **Dashboard** - Home with summary
- **My Assignments** - All your cases
- **Messages** - Communication (coming soon)
- **Settings** - Your profile and preferences

---

## Key Tips

- Check **"Needs Attention"** cases daily
- Review debtor's financial assessment before advising
- Use the case notes for your observations
- Your profile shows your initials/avatar in the sidebar
