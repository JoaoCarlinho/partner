# Admin Quick-Start Guide

**Time to read: 3 minutes**

Welcome to the Partner platform! As an administrator, you manage public defender invitations and oversee platform access.

---

## Your Main Tasks

1. **Invite public defenders** to join the platform
2. **Track invitation status** (pending, accepted, expired)
3. **Manage invitations** (resend or revoke as needed)

---

## Inviting a Public Defender

1. Navigate to **Admin > Defender Invitations**
2. Click **"Invite Defender"**
3. Enter the defender's:
   - Email address (required)
   - Organization name (optional)
4. Click **"Send Invitation"**

The defender receives an email with a secure registration link valid for 7 days.

---

## Checking Invitation Status

Your invitation dashboard shows:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** (yellow) | Invitation sent, awaiting acceptance |
| **Redeemed** (green) | Defender has registered |
| **Expired** (gray) | Link expired (can resend) |
| **Revoked** (red) | Invitation cancelled |

---

## Quick Actions

### Resend an Invitation
- Find the pending invitation
- Click **"Resend"**
- Confirms and extends expiration by 7 days

### Revoke an Invitation
- Find the pending invitation
- Click **"Revoke"**
- Defender can no longer use that link

---

## Summary Stats

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see:
- **Total** invitations sent
- **Pending** awaiting response
- **Redeemed** successfully registered
- **Expired** need attention
