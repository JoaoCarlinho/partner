# Paralegal Quick-Start Guide

**Time to read: 5 minutes**

Welcome to Partner! As a paralegal, you create and refine demand letters, then submit them to attorneys for approval.

---

## Your Main Tasks

1. **View and manage** demand letters for cases
2. **Edit letters** manually or with AI assistance
3. **Use AI refinement** to improve letter quality
4. **Submit letters** for attorney review
5. **Record payments** from debtors

---

## Viewing Demand Letters

1. Navigate to a **Case**
2. Select the **Demand Letters** tab
3. See all letters with status:
   - **Draft** - In progress, editable
   - **Pending Review** - Awaiting attorney
   - **Approved** - Ready to send
   - **Sent** - Delivered to debtor

---

## Editing a Letter

### Manual Editing

1. Click on a **Draft** letter
2. Edit text directly in the content area
3. Watch the **Compliance Panel** for issues
4. Click **Save** (or wait for auto-save)

### AI-Assisted Refinement

1. Open a draft letter
2. In the **Refinement Panel**, either:
   - Type your instruction (e.g., "Make the tone more professional")
   - Click a **Quick Suggestion** button
3. Wait for AI to process (~5 seconds)
4. Review the changes (shown as a diff)
5. Click **Accept** or **Reject**

**Popular Quick Actions:**
- "Make tone more professional"
- "Simplify language for readability"
- "Emphasize dispute rights"
- "Strengthen payment urgency"

---

## Version History

Every change creates a version. You can:
- **View** any previous version
- **Compare** two versions side-by-side
- **Undo/Redo** changes easily

---

## Submitting for Review

When your letter is ready:

1. Ensure compliance score is acceptable
2. Click **"Submit for Review"**
3. Letter status changes to **Pending Review**
4. Attorney is notified

> **Note**: You cannot submit if compliance score is below threshold.

---

## Recording Payments

When a debtor makes a payment:

1. Go to the case's **Payment Plans** section
2. Find the scheduled payment
3. Click **"Record Payment"**
4. Enter amount, date, and payment method
5. Save

---

## Key Tips

- **Auto-save** runs every 30 seconds while editing
- **Navigation warning** appears if you have unsaved changes
- **Compliance panel** updates in real-time as you edit
- **Version history** lets you recover from mistakes
