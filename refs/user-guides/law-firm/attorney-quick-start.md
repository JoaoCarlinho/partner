# Attorney Quick-Start Guide

**Time to read: 5 minutes**

Welcome to Partner! As an attorney, you review and approve demand letters, manage payment plan negotiations, and authorize communications.

---

## Your Main Tasks

1. **Review** demand letters submitted by paralegals
2. **Approve or reject** letters with feedback
3. **Sign** approved letters digitally
4. **Send** letters to debtors
5. **Review** debtor payment plan proposals
6. **Negotiate** and accept payment agreements

---

## Reviewing Demand Letters

1. Navigate to a **Case** or check your **Review Queue**
2. Letters marked **Pending Review** need your attention
3. Click to open and review:
   - Full letter content
   - Compliance status
   - Version history

---

## Approving a Letter

1. Review the letter content carefully
2. Check compliance score and any warnings
3. Click **"Approve"**
4. Optionally add your **digital signature**
5. Letter status changes to **Approved**

### Adding Your Signature

When approving, you can:
- Draw your signature on the signature pad
- Your signature is stored securely
- It appears on the final PDF sent to the debtor

---

## Rejecting a Letter

If changes are needed:

1. Click **"Reject"**
2. Enter your feedback (required, minimum 10 characters)
3. Be specific about what needs to change
4. Letter returns to **Draft** status
5. Paralegal is notified with your feedback

---

## Sending a Letter

After approval:

1. Click **"Prepare to Send"** (status: Ready to Send)
2. Preview the final PDF
3. Click **"Mark as Sent"** after delivery
4. Letter status changes to **Sent**

---

## Payment Plan Review

When debtors propose payment plans:

1. Go to case **Payment Plans** section
2. Review the proposal:
   - Total amount
   - Payment schedule
   - Debtor's financial assessment
3. Choose an action:
   - **Accept** - Agreement proceeds
   - **Reject** - Explain why
   - **Counter** - Propose different terms

### Making a Counter-Proposal

1. Click **"Counter"**
2. Adjust terms (amount, frequency, duration)
3. Add a message explaining your counter
4. Submit for debtor review

---

## Approval History

Every letter tracks:
- Who submitted it
- When you approved/rejected
- Your signature (if provided)
- All negotiation history

Access via the **Approval History** panel in letter details.

---

## Key Tips

- **PDF Preview** shows exactly what the debtor receives
- **Compliance flags** alert you to potential FDCPA issues
- **Version comparison** helps you see what changed since last review
- **Digital signatures** are legally binding and timestamped
